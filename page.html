Life

Life is a fundamental concept that distinguishes matter with biological processes from non-living matter. It encompasses characteristics such as homeostasis, organization, metabolism, growth, adaptation, response to stimuli, and reproduction. Life originated at least 3.5 billion years ago, leading to the diverse forms of life we see today. Philosophically, defining life has been challenging due to its process-based nature and the difficulty in distinguishing living entities from non-living ones.
